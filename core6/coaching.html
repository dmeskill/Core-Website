About

Here you will learn more about each or our coaches in CORE and the expectations for our Coaches. You will learn about their; Availability, what services they offer, and their experience in Overwatch. Please respect our Coaches as they are offering their time and knowledge to us.

Divisions

Coaches are set into different Divisions, coaches will range from Learning to Experts. Coaches will be dedicated to the teams in their Divisions. Coaches will move up in Divisions based on their Rank, Knowledge, Dedication and Approval by the Captains in the Divisions and Owners of CORE6.

  • Coaches are volunteering their time to help you improve, be respectful of it, and don't waste it
  • Our coaches are not police, counselors, or your parents. If you have issues with a player please speak with your/their captain about the issue
  • Our coaches will be responsible for being spectators for scims on one of our regularly scheduled days. If you want to scrim outside the regular days be sure to talk with the coach first. Be sure to give them a weeks advanced notice
  • Our coaches will help develop and recommend practices for both individuals and teams
  • Our coaches will review Vods and give feedback directly to players and teams
  • Our coaches will do "watch togethers" to review your game play with you and point out things to work on
  • Our coaches will give one on one advice to players on how to improve their overall skill
