Core

"CORE builds and supports curated Teams that are dedicated to helping each other improve."

  • We are looking for players who want to be part of a real team
  • We have a variety of teams with different goals and cultures
  • We offer support for teams who are in the build phase all the way to [D1] Division 1 Pro teams
  • We are all inclusive, the only things we discriminate against are bad attitudes and lack of commitment
  • We are not a politically correct group but we are respectful of one another
  • We aren't a "social club". While we know how to have fun our main focus is building successful teams
  • We expect that everyone here serves a purpose. If you are not contributing in some way there is no place for you here
  • We are an 18+ community
  • We don't put up with drama
  • We expect everyone to leave their sense of entitlement at the door

Offer

Our platform provides players and teams with the people and resources they need to succeed

  • [CAP] Captains give their team direction and confident leadership
  • [COACH] Coaches provide feedback to help players and teams improve
  • [TM] Team Mangers help our higher level teams with scheduling events and travel
  • THE FORGE: Weekly classes that teach players and teams how to get better at the game
  • And much much more!
